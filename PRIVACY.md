# Privacy Policy

This Chrome extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

The extension only writes data to the user's clipboard when the user clicks on a mailto: or tel: link. The clipboard is not read at any time.

No browsing activity is monitored or retained.

No data is sent to remote servers or third parties.

All behaviors occur locally on the user's device.
